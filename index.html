WHAT IS KOALATOON?

At Koalatoon, we are a community dedicated to reviving the environment through charity events and donations. We’re a huge believer in decentralization, and thus we have a community wallet funded by NFT sales where our holders can vote on and decide our next venture together. We are striving for a close-knit community where each and every member is important to the overall cause. Our goal is to eventually reach everyone across the world and create a lasting impact in society. At 0.08 ETH each and 5000 in total, owning a Koala gets you a unique PFP, exclusive merchandise, and your own part in world change!

RARITY

Our team made sure that each of the Koalatoon art is unique and easily distinguishable in the collection. The combination of Koalatoon aesthetics was randomly generated from over 150 hand drawn traits. This means no two Koalas are exactly alike.

FAQ

What is the total supply & mint price?
There will be a total of 5,000 Koalatoon available at a mint price of 0.08 ETH + gas fees.
When is the launch date?
We are launching the collection in June. Whitelist will mint first at 6pm EST and will have 4 hours to mint. The public sale will start at 10pm EST until we are sold out. The max txn/wallet will be 3 mints. Pre-whitelists will be given to the holders of our partnered project.
Why Koalatoon?
When you join us, you will have access to our close-knit community, exclusive access to the latest products, merchandise, events and more! On top of that, we are here to change not only the NFT space but the world as a whole.
How to obtain a Koalatoon NFT?
1. Download the metamask.io extension on your internet browser and sign up for a Metamask wallet.
2. Purchase Ethereum from various exchanges, such as Coinbase or Binance.
3. Send Ethereum from this exchange to your MetaMask wallet.
4. On launch day, open the Koalatoon website.
5. Click “MINT” button, Metamask will popup asking for connection.
6. Confirm the transaction and any associated fees.
7. Once you have made your purchase, your NFTs will appear in your wallet and on OpenSea.
