WHO ARE KOALA KREW?

Koala Krew is a community driven project of 5000 algorithmically generated NFTs on the Ethereum Blockchain, assembled from hundreds of visual attributes. The hand-drawn Koalas includes a variety of rare head, faces and outfits. Our goal is to reach everyone across the world and create a long lasting impact in the society. The price to purchase a Koala Krew NFT will cost 0.08 ETH each. By owning a Koala will earn you an amazing PFP, involvement to environmental change, exclusive access to the latest products, merchandise and events. Get your own to join the community!

RARITY

Our team made sure that each of the Koala Krew art is unique and easily distinguishable in the collection. The combination of Koala Krew aesthetics was randomly generated from over 150 hand drawn traits. This means no two Koalas are exactly alike.

There are 5 Koala Krew created by the artist that do not have randomly generated traits. The 5 Koala Krew NFTs are known as the most rare!

FAQ

What is the total supply & mint price?
There will be a total of 5,000 Koala Krew available at a mint price of 0.08 ETH + gas fees.
When is the launch date?
We are launching the collection on May 3rd. Whitelist will mint first at 6pm EST and will have 4 hours to mint. The public sale will start at 10pm EST until we are sold out. The max txn/wallet will be 3 mints. Pre-whitelists will be given to the holders of our partnered project.
Why Koala Krew?
When you join the krew, you will have access to our close-knit community, exclusive access to the latest products, merchandise, events and more! On top of that, we are here to change not only the NFT space but the world as a whole.
How to obtain a Koala Krew NFT?
1. Download the metamask.io extension on your internet browser and sign up for a Metamask wallet.
2. Purchase Ethereum from various exchanges, such as Coinbase or Binance.
3. Send Ethereum from this exchange to your MetaMask wallet.
4. On launch day, open the Koala Krew website.
5. Click “MINT” button, Metamask will popup asking for connection.
6. Confirm the transaction and any associated fees.
7. Once you have made your purchase, your NFTs will appear in your wallet and on OpenSea.
